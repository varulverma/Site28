  • Data Trust & Security: Data must be protected and traceable. Engineers embed security and quality checks into pipelines.

  • â€¢ Lack of Data Trust: Users lose confidence, leading to shadow IT and wasted effort.

How Leading Organizations Leverage Data Engineering
