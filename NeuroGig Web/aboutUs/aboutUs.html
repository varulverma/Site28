Our Technology Partner: Dremio

Powering the Next Generation of Data Analytics

At Neurongig, we are committed to leveraging the most innovative and robust technologies to deliver unparalleled value to our clients. That's why we are proud to partner with Dremio, the leader in the Open Data Lakehouse Platform.

This strategic partnership empowers us to deliver faster, more flexible, and more scalable data solutions, directly addressing the evolving needs of modern enterprises for analytics and AI.

Why Dremio? The Foundation for Future-Ready Data

Dremio transforms how organizations interact with their data, enabling a true self-service data experience directly on their data lake. Built on open-source technologies like Apache Iceberg and Apache Arrow, Dremio provides:

Blazing-Fast Query Performance

Dremio's patented query acceleration technologies, including Reflections, deliver sub-second query speeds for BI dashboards and interactive analytics, directly on your data lake.

Unified Data Access

Creates a powerful semantic layer that unifies data from diverse sources, providing a single, consistent view of all your data.

Open Data Lakehouse Architecture

Built natively for Apache Iceberg, ensuring your data remains in open formats and avoiding vendor lock-in.

Simplified Data Management

Automates data management tasks with Git-like versioning capabilities for robust change management.

AI-Ready Data Products

Designed to accelerate AI and ML initiatives by enabling creation of high-quality, governed data products.

Cost Efficiency

Significantly lowers TCO by eliminating data copies and optimizing query performance on low-cost storage.
